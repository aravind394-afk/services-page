Transparency & Accuracy

Editorial Policy

Read the editorial, review, correction and source-handling policy used for Hasthas laboratory equipment content.

Purpose

Hasthas publishes product pages and educational guides to help laboratory buyers understand equipment types, applications and selection factors before requesting a formal quotation.

Sources

Product specifications are based on the technical material supplied by Hasthas. Educational articles may also refer to recognised safety, public-health and environmental sources. External references are identified in the relevant article where applicable.

No invented specifications

When an exact specification, model number, price, certification scope or performance value is unavailable, it is not estimated. Visitors are directed to contact the company for the current configuration and quotation.

Review and updates

Content is reviewed for technical consistency, clear language, working internal links and current contact information. Pages may be updated when product configurations, supporting documents or guidance changes.

Corrections

Readers may report an error through the contact page. Material corrections are reviewed and updated as appropriate.

Commercial transparency

Prices are not displayed where they depend on capacity, construction, controls, accessories, quantity, tax, freight or installation. Any quotation is provided directly by Hasthas and should be treated as the current commercial reference.
