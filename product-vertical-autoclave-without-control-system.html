The supplied catalogue uses the common HSI-101 vertical-autoclave specification for both pictured versions. This card represents the version without the control system.

Product Information

Technical details and application

Review the available information and contact Hasthas to confirm the final model and commercial quotation.

Construction

Double-walled construction with stainless-steel boiler, inner chamber, outer chamber and thick stainless-steel lid with wing-nut locking.

Pressure and safety

Maximum pressure 30 psi; regular working pressure 15 psi; spring-loaded safety valve adjustable from 15 to 20 psi (+/- 3 psi); hydraulically tested up to 40 psi.

Heating and fittings

ISI-marked immersion heating element, pressure gauge, steam-release cock, safety valve and drain outlet.

Included supply

Pedal lifting device, perforated stainless-steel basket, wing-nut opener, cord and plug.

Electrical supply

220 V, 50 Hz AC.
