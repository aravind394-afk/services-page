Product Information

Technical details and application

Review the available information and contact Hasthas to confirm the final model and commercial quotation.

Construction

Triple-walled construction with stainless-steel inner chamber, powder-coated mild-steel outer chamber and adjustable stainless-steel perforated trays.

Insulation

A 3-inch gap between the walls is filled with special-grade glass wool to reduce heat loss.

Door

Double-walled door with stainless-steel inner panel, powder-coated mild-steel outer panel, heavy stainless-steel hinges and spring-loaded lock.

Heating

High-grade nichrome heating elements are mounted in porcelain beads at the bottom and both vertical sides for uniform heating.

Temperature control

Capillary thermostat from 5°C above ambient to 250°C with +/- 2°C sensitivity.

Ventilation and supply

Top and bottom ventilators; supplied for 220 V, single-phase, 50 Hz AC operation.
