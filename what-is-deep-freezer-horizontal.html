What Is a Horizontal Deep Freezer? Uses and Applications

Learn what a Deep Freezer Horizontal is, how it works, its common uses, key specifications and the details to check before requesting a quotation.

Written by Hasthas Technical TeamTechnical review: Hasthas Scientific InstrumentsLast reviewed 18 July 2026
Quick answer

Top-opening horizontal deep freezer for medical, R&D, agricultural, educational and industrial applications. It is commonly selected for low-temperature storage of compatible laboratory samples, reagents and materials in a chest-style cabinet layout. The listed Hasthas model is HSI-109.

What is a Deep Freezer Horizontal?

Top-opening horizontal deep freezer for medical, R&D, agricultural, educational and industrial applications.

Low-temperature storage of compatible laboratory samples, reagents and materials in a chest-style cabinet layout.

How does a Deep Freezer Horizontal work?

A refrigeration system removes heat from an insulated chest chamber, helping retain cold air when the top lid is opened.

Common laboratory uses

Low-temperature storage of compatible laboratory samples, reagents and materials in a chest-style cabinet layout.

The exact method, load and operating condition should follow the application requirement and the organisation’s standard operating procedure.

Key specifications at a glance

  • Ambient +5°C to -20°C
  • 110-280 L listed
  • Digital PT100 control
  • Construction: Double-walled, top-opening chamber with polished stainless-steel top, lid and inner chamber; powder-coated mild-steel outer body.
  • Insulation: Combination of special-grade glass wool and PUF insulation between the walls to minimise sweating in humid conditions.
  • Door and sealing: Top lid with gasket seals and locking arrangement.
  • Cooling and control: Evaporating coils, compressor, air-cooled condenser and heavy-duty fan with digital temperature controller-cum-indicator and PT100 sensor.
  • Temperature and supply: Ambient +5°C to -20°C; 220/240 V, single-phase, 50 Hz AC.

Available sizes, models and options

Available capacities:

  • 110 L · 550 x 400 x 650 mm
  • 165 L · 800 x 400 x 650 mm
  • 280 L · 1200 x 400 x 650 mm

Optional features listed for the product:

  • Voltage stabilizer
