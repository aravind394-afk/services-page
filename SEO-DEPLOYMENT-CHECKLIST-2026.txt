HASHTHAS SEO DEPLOYMENT CHECKLIST

1. Confirm the final live domain. Replace every occurrence of https://hasthasscientificinstruments.in if a custom domain will be used.
2. Deploy the complete folder, including _redirects, _headers, robots.txt and sitemap.xml.
3. Add and verify the final domain property in Google Search Console.
4. Submit /sitemap.xml in Search Console.
5. Inspect the home page, products page, top category pages and priority product/blog URLs, then request indexing.
6. Create or fully complete the Google Business Profile with the same company name, address, phone, website and product categories.
7. Add genuine customer reviews and current business photos to the Google Business Profile.
8. Test key pages in PageSpeed Insights after deployment and optimize any hosting-specific issues.
9. Test structured data with Google Rich Results Test and Schema.org Validator. Product pages without public prices are designed for quotation enquiries and may not qualify for merchant rich results.
10. Build legitimate citations/backlinks from suppliers, colleges, industry directories, associations and customer/partner websites. Avoid paid spam links.
11. Track Search Console queries and leads monthly. Expand pages only when real search-query data shows a useful gap.
12. Keep specifications, images, phone, email and availability accurate. Update sitemap lastmod only when content truly changes.
