IMPORTANT
A 95+ Lighthouse SEO score is a realistic technical target after deployment, but no HTML change can guarantee a specific score or a #1 Google ranking. Rankings also depend on domain authority, indexing, backlinks, Google Business Profile strength, reviews, competitor activity, location, content performance and time.

The meta keywords tag is included because it was requested, but Google does not use meta keywords as a ranking signal. The implementation relies on useful visible content, internal linking, clean metadata, crawlability and structured data instead of keyword stuffing.

Google no longer displays FAQ rich results for normal commercial websites as of 2026. The visible FAQs remain useful for users and topical coverage; existing FAQ schema has been kept synchronized where already present, without relying on a rich-result promise.
