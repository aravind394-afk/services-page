What Is a Horizontal Laminar Flow Cabinet? Uses and Applications

Learn what a Laminar Flow Cabinet Horizontal is, how it works, its common uses, key specifications and the details to check before requesting a quotation.

Written by Hasthas Technical TeamTechnical review: Hasthas Scientific InstrumentsLast reviewed 18 July 2026
Quick answer

Horizontal laminar-flow workstation for clean-air laboratory procedures. It is commonly selected for creating a clean, filtered horizontal airflow across the work surface to protect suitable non-hazardous samples and processes from contamination. The listed Hasthas model is HSI-128.

What is a Laminar Flow Cabinet Horizontal?

Horizontal laminar-flow workstation for clean-air laboratory procedures.

Creating a clean, filtered horizontal airflow across the work surface to protect suitable non-hazardous samples and processes from contamination.

How does a Laminar Flow Cabinet Horizontal work?

A blower passes air through a high-efficiency filter and directs clean air horizontally across the work surface toward the operator side.

Common laboratory uses

Creating a clean, filtered horizontal airflow across the work surface to protect suitable non-hazardous samples and processes from contamination.

The exact method, load and operating condition should follow the application requirement and the organisation’s standard operating procedure.

Key specifications at a glance

  • Class 100 condition
  • HEPA filtration
  • 90 ft/min airflow
  • Clean-air performance: Designed to provide particle-free air meeting Class 100 conditions of the US Federal Standard.
  • Filtration: Pre-filter plus HEPA filter rated 99.99% with cold DOP and 99.97% with hot DOP for particles 0.3 micron and larger.
  • Air velocity: Designed laminar airflow velocity of 90 ft/min +/- 20%.
  • Construction: Duro-board cabinet with laminated exterior, epoxy-painted interior, optional stainless-steel worktop and transparent side panels.
  • Blower performance: Direct-drive blower with 1/4 HP motor; noise below 65 dB and vibration below 2.5 micrometres, as listed.
  • Electrical supply: 230 V, single-phase, 50 Hz AC.
